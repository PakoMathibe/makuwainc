MAKUWA INC LEGAL SOLUTIONS

COMPANY INTRODUCTION AND HISTORY

FOUNDED IN 2017

MAKUWA INC LEGAL SOLUTIONS was founded in 2017 by Thabo Makuwa with over 6 (Six) Years working experience with the private and public Sector. The founder is currently the member of the Council for Debt Counsellor of South Africa in Good Standing and he holds an LLB Honours Degree, Certificate for Mediation and Arbitration.

FAST GROWWING

MAKUWA INC LEGAL SOLUTIONS is also a Fast growing Black owned legal consulting firm based in Pretoria and representation throughout South Africa. Combining over ten years’ experience in various areas of law amongst others ,Commercial law, insurance law ,Labour law ,construction law, Debt Collection, Consumer Matters and gives the consulting firm an advantage to service its clients diversified needs.

NEW GENERATION

As a new generation legal consulting firm, we have encountered the challenges of empowerment and transformation by taking a leading role through the creation of a new generation multi-disciplinary legal consulting firm that addresses the needs of their clients following a holistic approach. We acknowledge diversity and equal opportunity as key issues for meaningful and sustainable participation in the Broad –Base Economic Empowerment initiative for this purpose; the legal consulting firm has a well-established transformation policy.

ABOUT MAKUWA INC LEGAL SOLUTIONS

  • OUR TEAM
  • SERVICES
  • OUR CLIENTS

With one Director, an Attorney, Two Advocates and one office and marketing manager MAKUWA INC LEGAL SOLUTIONS is a well-established, medium-sized business legal consulting firm with significant national capacity through a network of correspondence and associates, giving us sizable influence in key financial centres’ through the province and the Republic of SA.

We offer a full range of corporate and commercial legal services, including but not limited to, Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR), Mediation and Arbitration (divorce, debt, criminal and labour), BEE Consulting Services, Litigation, Employment, Company Restructuring, Real Estate, Criminal Law, Contract Law, Motor Vehicle Accidents (MVA) and Estate Matters.

The legal consulting firm’s client base includes local and national individuals and companies across a broad range of sectors, ranging from emerging business, and public sector population.
